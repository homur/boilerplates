About

oh hi!

Blackfish is a sole proprietorship owned and run by Onur Atalay, who works for agencies and companies in Turkey as designer and Front-end developer for more than 10 years now. Born in May 1983, He studied French Interpretation as the license graduation. He has been working in the digital advertising industry since 2006 and since then, he has worked with some of the biggest companies in Turkey such as Paşabahce, Turknet, Şişecam and two major global brands, Dove and Vaseline.
